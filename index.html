Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AutoTrader

How accurate is the AI valuation?

Our AI analyzes over 10 million data points including current market trends, vehicle condition, location, and historical sales data. The accuracy rate is 95%+ with valuations typically within $500 of actual sale prices.

Is my personal data secure?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption, are GDPR compliant, and never share your data with third parties without explicit consent. All transactions are secured with bank-level encryption.

How quickly can I sell my car?

On average, cars listed on AutoTrader sell 40% faster than traditional methods. Most listings receive offers within 24-48 hours, with complete sales typically closing within 7 days.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees, ever. Our pricing is transparent: free valuations, free listings for buyers, and sellers pay only a small commission upon successful sale. No upfront costs or subscription fees.

Which countries are supported?

We currently operate in 45+ countries across North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia. Our platform supports 30+ languages and handles local currency conversions automatically.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! Our mobile apps for iOS and Android offer the full desktop experience with additional features like instant notifications, AR vehicle preview, and one-tap messaging. Download from App Store or Google Play.

What kind of support do you offer?

24/7 customer support via live chat, email, and phone. Our expert team typically responds within 5 minutes during business hours. We also offer video consultations for premium users.

Do you verify vehicle history?

Yes, every listing includes a comprehensive vehicle history report covering accidents, ownership, service records, and title status. We partner with leading verification services to ensure accuracy.
